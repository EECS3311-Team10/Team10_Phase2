# ConsultLink Platform Policies

## 1. Booking & Availability
* **Booking Requests:** All bookings begin in a `Requested` state. A booking is only finalized once a Consultant moves it to the `Confirmed` state.
* **Consultant Rights:** Consultants have the authority to accept or reject any booking request based on their current schedule and expertise.
* **Double Booking:** The system automatically prevents a consultant from being booked for the same time slot by multiple clients.
* **Services Offered:** Consulting Intro session, Resume Review and Mock interview.

## 2. Cancellation & Refund Policy
* **Standard Cancellation:** Clients can cancel a booking at any time via their dashboard.
* **Refund Eligibility:** * Cancellations made more than **24 hours** before the scheduled session are eligible for a 100% refund.
* Cancellations made within **24 hours** of the session are subject to a 50% "Late Cancellation Fee."
* **Consultant Cancellation:** If a Consultant cancels a `Paid` booking, the Client always receives a 100% refund.

## 3. Payment Policy
* **Workflow:** Once a booking is `Confirmed`, it moves to `Pending Payment`. The session cannot occur until the status is updated to `Paid`.
* **Accepted Methods:** The platform simulates payments for Credit Card, Debit Card, PayPal, and Bank Transfer.
* **Security:** All payment information is stored as simulated tokens. No real financial data is ever processed or stored by ConsultLink.

## 4. Pricing Strategy
* **Base Rates:** Each consulting service has a fixed base price determined by the platform administrator.
* **Urgency Surcharge:** A 15% surcharge may be applied to bookings requested within 6 hours of the session start time.

## 5. Session Completion
* **Finalization:** A booking is marked as `Completed` only after the consultant confirms the session has occurred and the payment is fully processed.
* **Disputes:** If a session does not occur but is marked as completed, clients must contact the platform administrator within 48 hours.

## 6. AI Assistant Guidelines
* **Role:** The AI assistant provides general information about ConsultLink and guides users through common workflows.
* **Limitations:** To ensure security, the AI cannot process payments, cancel bookings, or access private user data. It is for informational support only.
